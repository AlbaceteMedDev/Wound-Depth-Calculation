How to Use the Conformable Drape Method

        
    
            
  1. Lay a sterile surgical drape over the wound opening. Press it gently so it conforms to the entire wound bed — into the full depth, tunnels, undermined edges, and all irregular surfaces.
    2. 
            
  2. Trace the wound margin on the drape with a surgical marking pen while it's still in the wound.
    3. 
            
  3. Remove the drape carefully and lay it flat on a sterile surface.
    4. 
            
  4. Measure the area inside the traced outline (overlay on graph paper or use a planimetry tool). Measure the perimeter of the outline.
    5. 
            
  5. Enter the values into this calculator. The 0.5 cm fixation border for graft anchoring is added automatically.
